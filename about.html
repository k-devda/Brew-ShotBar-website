Who We Are

Born From a Passion for Perfection

Brew & Shots was born in 2018 from a simple but powerful idea: what if the warmth of a great coffee shop could coexist with the electric energy of a premium bar? Our founders, Aryan and Meera, spent years travelling the world — from the espresso bars of Milan to the cocktail lounges of Tokyo — before bringing their vision to life in the heart of Bengaluru.

What started as a 20-seat café on MG Road has grown into a 200-seat luxury destination, complete with a full bar, gourmet kitchen, and the city's most talked-about sports viewing lounge. Every detail — from the hand-selected coffee beans to the custom-designed furniture — reflects our obsession with quality.

To create a space where every guest feels like a VIP — where the quality of every cup, every cocktail, and every bite reflects our unwavering commitment to excellence. We exist to turn ordinary moments into extraordinary memories.

Our Journey

The Brew & Shots Story

2018

The Beginning

Aryan and Meera open a 20-seat café on MG Road with just 3 coffee machines and a dream. The first week sells out every day.

2019

The Bar is Born

We expand to the adjacent space and launch our premium bar section. The concept of "coffee meets cocktails" takes Bengaluru by storm.

2020

Surviving & Thriving

Despite the pandemic, we launch our delivery service and online ordering system. Our loyal community keeps us alive through the toughest year.

2021

The Watch Lounge

We install our first giant screen and launch the Watch Party concept. The IPL season brings in record crowds and puts us on the map.

2022

Full Renovation

Complete redesign of the space — 200 seats, 4 giant screens, a dedicated cocktail bar, and a gourmet kitchen. The new Brew & Shots is born.

2023

Award Season

Times Food Award for Best Coffee Bar. Zomato's #1 rated café in Bengaluru. Featured in Condé Nast Traveller India's "Best Bars" list.

2025

The Future

Expanding to Mumbai and Hyderabad. Launching our own coffee bean brand. Building the most premium sports viewing experience in India.
