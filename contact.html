FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept walk-ins? +

Yes, we welcome walk-ins! However, we strongly recommend reservations on Friday and Saturday evenings, and during major sporting events, as we tend to fill up quickly.

What is the minimum age for the bar area? +

The bar area is strictly 21+ after 9 PM. Valid government-issued ID is required. The coffee shop and restaurant areas are open to all ages throughout the day.

Can I book for a private event? +

Absolutely! We offer private event bookings for birthdays, corporate events, and special occasions. Contact us at events@brewandshots.in for custom packages and pricing.

Which sports events do you screen? +

We screen all major cricket matches (IPL, World Cup, Test series), football (Premier League, Champions League, ISL), Formula 1, tennis Grand Slams, and boxing events.

Is there a dress code? +

Smart casual is preferred, especially in the evenings. We ask guests to avoid beachwear, torn clothing, or sports jerseys in the bar area after 8 PM.

Do you offer home delivery? +

Yes! We deliver coffee, snacks, and select food items within a 5km radius. You can also order through Zomato and Swiggy for wider delivery coverage.
